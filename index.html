Pampering Services

Make your appointment with us for all your gooming needs. We make the effort to honor all special requests and needs. Just ask us! All on our team have special training and expertise.

  • Eye and Ear Cleaning

    Our professional eye and ear cleaning is thorough and gentle to ensure your pet enjoys a comfortable and healthy experience.

  • Flea and Tick Treatment

    We can help you protect your fur babies from pesky parasites with our effective flea and tick treatments.

  • Nail Clipping/Grinding

    Experience the utmost care and precision as we expertly handle nail clipping and grinding for your beloved pets, for light or dark colored toenails.

  • Puppy/Kitten Pedicures

    This service ensures tiny paws are well-cared for and ready for endless play time.

  • Bath, Shampoo, Blow Dry

    All fur types welcome. We use cutting-edge shampoo formulas for deshedding. Sensitive skin and allergies can be relieved using our special shampoo formulas.

  • Groom to Breed Standard

    From Afghan Hounds to Persian cats, and all breeds in between, we can get your pet show-ready.
